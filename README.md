The objective is to: 
● Predict which customers are at high risk of churn in the near future 
● Understand why they are likely to churn – e.g., poor service experience, billing shocks, 
price dissatisfaction, low engagement 
● Design targeted offers and interventions (retention actions) to reduce churn and protect 
customer lifetime value (CLV) 
The project will use customer-level data such as demographics, plan details, usage patterns 
(voice, data, SMS), payment behaviour, contract type, complaint and service interaction history, 
and tenure with the operator.
